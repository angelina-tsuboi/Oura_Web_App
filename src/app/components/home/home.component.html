Oura

Oura is a tailorable, portable, and inexpensive indoor air quality (IAQ) monitoring device with customizable features for the immunocompromised and those living in vulnerable areas. Oura also includes a complimentary app that utilizes machine learning to tailor the detector to its user based on health conditions and sends air quality notifications to any of the user's caregivers.
